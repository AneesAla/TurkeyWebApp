Introduction: Building the Foundations of Resilient Cities

As global urban populations continue to rise, the challenges associated with creating livable, sustainable, and resilient cities come into focus. United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 11 (SDG 11) aims to ensure that cities and human settlements are inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. The recent earthquakes in Turkey have underscored the dire consequences of non-compliance with building safety regulations, showing off the critical need for through urban planning and infrastructure development. This section outlines the importance of aligning urban development strategies with SDG 11 to mitigate the impacts of such disasters and to foster sustainable growth in urban environments.
